# The Shift Worker

### Shift work, solved.

**The Problem**
Complexity is the weapon of the exploiter. Inaccurate tracking, lost overtime, and schedule volatility cost the global shift workforce billions annually.

**The Solution**
The Shift Worker is a high-precision utility designed for the modern laborer. It provides a clean, authoritative interface to track hours, calculate exact pay, and manage life around the clock. No fluff. Just data that works for you.

**Key Features**
- **Automated Verification:** Cross-reference scheduled hours against actual clock-ins with millisecond precision.
- **Dynamic Pay Forecasting:** Know your earnings before the paycheck arrives. Tax, overtime, and differentials included.
- **Temporal Sovereignty:** Tools to defend your time off and optimize your recovery cycles.
- **Schedule Intelligence:** Built for rotating shifts, swing shifts, and night work.
- **Private by Default:** Your data stays yours. No employer access. No third-party sharing.
